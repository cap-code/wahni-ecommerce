Franchise

The company aims to have a total of 200 access points in Kerala within the next one year and 3000 access points within another year. That is, a customer should not be more than 5km away from his support point. 3000 will be the saturation point. We won't allow more franchise than that. Similarly, the 200 franchise is an interim saturation point. Until all the 200 franchises become sustainable we won't more forward to the 3000-franchise target.

Applicants will be shortlisted only after reviewing the applications and talking to the applicants at least twice. After that, we start training them for a small fee. The first stage is a five-week class with one class per week. Assignments will be given between these classes. Once it is completed, the style and content of the subsequent classes will be determined. There will also be special follow-up classes for those interested but unable to run along. The franchise agreement will be awarded to those who successfully complete the training.

The first three months will be a probationary period even if the franchise agreement is completed. Franchises will be reviewed every six months. Steps will be taken to ensure quality accordingly. We always put customer interest ahead.
